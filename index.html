About Danvepa

Your Production Partner
in Nairobi

From branded uniforms and promotional gifts to signage, stationery and annual reports — Danvepa handles every production detail under one roof.

We partner with marketing teams, procurement departments and project managers to deliver branded materials — from a single business card reorder to a full-organisation uniform rollout across multiple locations.

With five core product categories, we are the single partner that handles everything. No coordinating between vendors. No quality surprises. Just results — on brief, on deadline, every time.

Because when the brand is right, the organisation is trusted.
And that is our only measure of success.

What We Produce

Our Product Categories

Eight service categories covering every branded touchpoint your organisation needs — all under one roof.

Apparels & Branded Uniforms

Staff uniforms, workwear and branded clothing — screen printed or embroidered to your exact specification for any industry.

  • Polo shirts, T-shirts & dress shirts
  • Coveralls, lab coats & dust coats
  • Hi-vis vests & safety wear
  • Caps, bucket hats & hard hats
  • Hoodies, tracksuits & sports kits
  • Lanyards & ID holders

Stationery & Office Items

Premium branded stationery for daily corporate use — from first impressions to every internal touchpoint.

  • Business cards & letterheads
  • Notebooks, diaries & journals
  • Presentation folders & files
  • Branded pens & desk accessories
  • Envelopes, notepads & calendars
  • Mouse pads & ID card holders

Outdoor Advertising & Signage

High-impact branded displays for events, offices, streets and vehicles — designed to make your presence undeniable.

  • Roll-up & X-frame banners
  • Pop-up displays & flex banners
  • Teardrop flags & branded flags
  • Vehicle & fleet branding
  • Outdoor & indoor signage boards
  • Promotional counters & tables

Promotional Items & Corporate Gifts

Branded gifts and giveaways that keep your organisation top of mind — for events, client appreciation and award ceremonies.

  • Mugs, tumblers & travel flasks
  • Tote bags, canvas & jute bags
  • Keyrings, power banks & wristbands
  • Umbrellas, clocks & photo frames
  • Plaques, trophies & awards
  • Desk organisers & gift sets

Printing & Publications

Professional document design and print production for corporates, NGOs and government organisations — from a single report to a full annual print run.

  • Brochures & flyers
  • Annual & financial reports
  • Brand & print solutions catalogues
  • Invoice & receipt books
  • Tender document production
  • Posters & promotional print

Design Services

Creative design that builds a consistent, memorable brand identity — from the first logo to every printed or digital asset your team needs.

  • Logo & brand identity design
  • Brand guidelines & style manuals
  • Marketing collateral & templates
  • Packaging & label design
  • Presentation & pitch deck design
  • Artwork preparation for print

Social Media Management

Strategic content creation and community management that keeps your brand active, consistent and growing across all major platforms.

  • Content strategy & calendar planning
  • Branded graphics & video reels
  • Facebook, Instagram & LinkedIn
  • Page setup & profile optimisation
  • Community management & DM handling
  • Monthly performance reports

Web Design & Software Solutions

Modern websites and tailored business software that streamline how your organisation operates, communicates and grows.

  • Corporate & portfolio websites
  • E-commerce & online stores
  • CRM systems & client portals
  • Inventory & stock management
  • HR & payroll software
  • Custom business dashboards
